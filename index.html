Conductor

The magic finger
to conduct your TV.

With Conductor, You can...

気になったものの
情報を得る

テレビにうつった
商品を買う

テレビ画面を
意のままに操る

Search

Circle the person

you want information about.

M-shaped you write,

so you can get the information

　　　　about the music playing.

Purchase

Check out the item

you want to buy, and it will be

added to your shopping list.

（Conductor searches lowest price of the item　
on Amazon, Rakuten & Yahoo!Shopping.）

Control

Pinch IN/OUT when

　　you want to zoom the screen.

Move your hand

　　　　　　forward to pause the TV.

　　　　and move it once more,　　

　　　　　　　　　　　　to resume it.
